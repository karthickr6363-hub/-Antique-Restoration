Our Story

Three generations of craftsmanship dedicated to preserving history's finest treasures

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2001 by master craftsman James Mitchell, our restoration shop began as a small family workshop dedicated to preserving the beauty of antique furniture. What started as a passion project has grown into one of the most respected restoration studios in the region.

Today, we continue the tradition of excellence that James established, combining time-honored techniques with modern conservation science. Each piece that enters our workshop tells a story, and we consider it our privilege to give these stories new life.

From Victorian armchairs to Art Deco cabinets, our team has restored over 1,250 pieces, each with its own unique challenges and rewards. We don't just repair furniture; we revive memories, preserve heritage, and create legacies for future generations.
